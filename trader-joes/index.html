Trader Joe's NYC Location Analysis

Identifying Optimal Locations for New Stores

Overview

This data science project identifies the best locations for Trader Joe's to open new stores in New York City. The analysis combines demographic data, transit accessibility, and retail competition to score potential locations across the five boroughs.

The recommendation is based on similarity scores calculated from multiple factors including population density, competition from other grocery chains, transit accessibility, educational attainment, and income levels.

Key Findings

  • Trader Joe's locations strongly correlate with transit accessibility
  • Stores cluster in areas with high education levels and medium-high population density
  • Several underserved areas match the demographic profile of successful existing locations
  • ZIP code 10038 in Manhattan (Two Bridges) emerged as a top recommendation
